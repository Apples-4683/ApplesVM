## Choose Your Architecture
Before you start, open **Control Panel > System and Security > System**. Check your "System type" carefully.

| Architecture | Guide Link | Best For... |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **64-Bit (x86_64)** | [**Go to x64 Guide**](Documentation/Windows7SP3x64.md) | Modern VMs, Gaming, and 4GB+ RAM systems. |
| **32-Bit (x86)** | [**Go to x86 Guide**](Documentation/Windows7SP3x86.md) | Legacy hardware, Netbooks, and Vintage CPUs. Usually this. |
